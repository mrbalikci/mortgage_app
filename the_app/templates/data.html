Glossary

  • Married - Marital status of the individual applicant
  • Dependents - Applicant's number of legal dependents
  • Education - Signifies whether the applicant has earned a college degree
  • Self Employed - Applicant works for independently rather than an employer
  • Applicant Income - Applicant's monthly income
  • Coapplicant Income - Coapplicant's monthly income (if applicable)
  • Loan Amount - The principal of the loan (in $ thousands)
  • Loan Amount Term - The number of terms (in months)
  • Credit History - Signifies whether the applicant has a credit score above 640
  • Property Area - The development level of the area the applicant plans to purchase
  • Loan Status - Signifies whether the loan was approved or denied
